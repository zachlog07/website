About Us

Rivera Construction & Hardscapes LLC is a full-service construction and hardscaping company based in Lebanon, CT. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service. Our team is committed to turning your vision into a reality, ensuring a fast, efficient, and stress-free experience from start to finish. We pride ourselves on our reliability, attention to detail, and creating beautiful, lasting spaces for our clients.
